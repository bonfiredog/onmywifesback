CHAT MESSAGES FOR PIRATEBOX
===========================

29th May, 1853: Crin ld by Cptn & Cmmdr ISAAK SCINBANK of H.P.S. Otranto. Much well, othr nt impt. Encmpd here to trace game fr four (4) days, and then mking NNE for the contunce of mssn. Fair lol & mch brak of ice. To ne englishman to pass, relay nws to Lndn. By our gd, GD   

2nd June, 1853: Fnal msg afor dprtur. Nun prishd, bt game illusv. Plz do nt mlest crin of all our dgnty - we may av nd fit. By our gd, GD
